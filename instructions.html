DARK MODE

Imagine your keyboard has a raised bump on the F and J keys. These bumps serve as a guide for your index fingers. Here's where each finger rests on the home row:

Left Hand

  • Pinky finger on the A key
  • Ring finger on the S key
  • Middle finger on the D key
  • Index finger on the F key (with the bump)
  • Thumb rests on the spacebar

Right Hand

  • Pinky finger on the semicolon (;) key
  • Ring finger on the L key
  • Middle finger on the K key
  • Index finger on the J key (with the bump)
  • Thumb rests on the spacebar

Reaching Other Keys

Without moving your hands from the home row, use the following fingers to reach other keys

  • Pinky: Reaches for keys like Tab, Shift, Caps Lock, Enter, and the bottom row keys (Ctrl, Alt, etc.) on its side.
  • Ring Finger: Reaches for keys like Z, X, C, V, and the symbols above them.
  • Middle Finger: Reaches for keys like W, E, R, T, and the symbols above them.
  • Index Finger: Reaches for the top row keys (numbers and symbols) above their home row keys (F and J).
